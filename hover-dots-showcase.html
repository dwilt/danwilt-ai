HOVER.FX :: DOT VARIATIONS

Hover over each button to see the effect. Small dots positioned under the bottom-left of the text label, quickly flashing in and out. Each variation has a different timing personality.

A :: STAGGERED BLINK
Three dots blink in rapid succession with staggered delays. Clean and rhythmic — like a status indicator cycling through states. 0.6s loop.
